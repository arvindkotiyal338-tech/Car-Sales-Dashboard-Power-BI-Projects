# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive **Car Sales Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze sales performance across multiple dimensions such as time, region, company, and product attributes.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to track key metrics and uncover actionable insights for better decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance (YTD & MTD)  
- Identify top-performing products, regions, and companies  
- Track sales trends over time  
- Understand customer preferences (body style, color)  
- Support data-driven business strategies  

---

## 📊 Key Features
- 📈 YTD Sales Analysis with growth %  
- 🚘 Cars Sold & Average Price Tracking  
- 📊 Weekly Sales Trend Visualization  
- 🧩 Body Style & Color Distribution  
- 🌍 Region-wise Sales Map  
- 🏢 Company-wise Performance Analysis  
- 🔍 Dynamic Filters (Slicers) for deep dive analysis  

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Achieved **$371M+ YTD sales** with **23% growth**  
- **SUV segment** is the highest contributor to total revenue  
- **Pale White** is the most preferred car color  
- **Chevrolet, Ford, and Dodge** are top-performing companies  
- Slight decline in **average price (-0.79%)** indicates pricing pressure  
- Certain regions contribute significantly, while others show growth potential
